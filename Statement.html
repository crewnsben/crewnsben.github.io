We shape our buildings; thereafter they shape us.
We shape our technology; thereafter it shapes us.

What if we made technology accessible, to then shape our buildings and eventually shape us ?

This project investigates the creation of accessible & multimodal communication tools, catering to either residents and architects/developers in the city of LA.

The goal of this platform is to enrich the communication between all involved parties for maters of architecture, public space and planning/policy. The software is able to provide summary and information on policy and regulations based on public data from the web, visualize it and eventually facilitate the rapid prototyping of projects of varied scale.
